"The World of Sally" features a technologically adept guide, Sally, who incorporates visceral interfaces and magic technology into her vibration integration ritual. This work showcases networked and integrated cyber chemistry, breaking the boundaries between virtual and physical realms. It offers strong sensory experiences not only in terms of visual presentation but also through sound performance and interactive technology.

Xin uses 3D modelling, printing, and sculpture to help build this magical world.
