In' Bug Mod’ you can make, tattoo and photograph a digital pet insect. In-game character creation lets players customise a mass-produced product, allowing it to metamorphose into something they can form an intimate relationship with. Bug Mod centres character creation as the core and exclusive mechanic, a small world where players can make a digital creature and personalise it by giving it tattoos.

Inspired both by freely made and distributed aesthetic mod packs and free to play web hosted dress up games, bug mod replaces super clean digital aesthetics with something grimier, something that wriggles and squirms away.

This game is a collaborative effort between George and me.

*Unreal Engine
*Zbrush
*Blender
*Adobe Substance 3D Painter
