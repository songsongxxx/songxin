BUG MOD

𝒱𝒾𝒹𝑒𝑜 𝑔𝒶𝓂𝑒

In' Bug Mod’ you can make, tattoo and photograph a digital pet insect. In-game character creation lets players customise a mass-produced product, allowing it to metamorphose into something they can form an intimate relationship with. Bug Mod centres character creation as the core and exclusive mechanic, a small world where players can make a digital creature and personalise it by giving it tattoos.

Inspired both by freely made and distributed aesthetic mod packs and free to play web hosted dress up games, bug mod replaces super clean digital aesthetics with something grimier, something that wriggles and squirms away.

This game is a collaborative effort between George and xin.

*Unreal Engine
*Zbrush
*Blender
*Adobe Substance 3D Painter

The exhibition at Kunstpunkt Berlin Galerie für zeitgenössische Kunst includes the group Kaugummi im Motherboard, curated by Katya Quel and Yi Li. In Berlin’s digital landscape, this project explores the interplay of digital art, gaming culture, and post-internet aesthetics. By transforming the exhibition space, "Gum in the Motherboard" invites audiences to engage with contemporary digital discourse, merging philosophical insights with practical realities to navigate the complexities of life in the digital age. Supported by Kunstpunkt Berlin and Part of Sellery Weekend.
